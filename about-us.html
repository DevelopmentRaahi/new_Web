Flexible, expert,
& powered for progression

Freshminds is reshaping the future of resourcing and consulting. We are committed to helping power growth by connecting excellence and expertise, on-demand.

We have been a pioneer of the contingent workforce model for decades. By activating our global network of the highest-calibre individuals, we bridge the gap between business needs and required skills for commercial, strategic and analytical challenges.

We are agile

Founded in 2000, Freshminds is a dynamic collection of talents and opportunities. Powered by people, from analyst to expert, we’re plugged-in to a world of skills, knowledge and experience.

As the world of work evolves, from on-demand skill sets and flexible working, we continue to create real impact and help meet business challenges, with an outlook that's always focused on the future.

We are connected

By making the right connections, we help ambitious companies and people effect significant growth – for a day, a month, a year or forever. No wonder we’re trusted by globally recognised companies to fast-scaling start-ups, in professional services, finance, retail, tech and beyond.

What we stand for

Partnership

Collaboration is at the heart of our service. By developing meaningful connections, we can build long-lasting relationships to truly understand obstacles, drivers, and overall strategy.

Courageous

We always aim to be decisive. Challenging ourselves and those around us means we can advise on the best possible solutions for both clients and candidates.

Drive

Our ambitious attitude means we continually strive to improve. It gives us momentum to challenge industry norms, driving innovation and pushing us closer to our goals and those of our partners.

Excellence

It’s our service promise, our people promise and our delivery promise. With our credible industry knowledge, a proven reputation for delivering outstanding work and a highly-skilled network, excellence is at the heart of everything we do.
