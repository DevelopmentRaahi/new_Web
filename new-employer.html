    Scaling Tech Hiring for a Growing SaaS Company

    Requirement

    The client needed to expand its engineering team quickly to support new product development and client integrations. They were hiring for roles such as Software Engineers, Frontend Developers, and QA Engineers within a strict 45-day timeline.

    Challenges Faced

    • High competition for skilled tech talent.
    • Low response rate from passive candidates.
    • Internal team lacked time for detailed screening and technical assessments.

    How We Helped

    To meet these challenges, we built a specialized tech hiring strategy tailored to the client’s requirements. We started by conducting a detailed role analysis with the engineering manager to identify must-have vs. good-to-have skills.

    Our sourcing team used advanced Boolean searches, GitHub and Stack Overflow profiling, and referral networks to identify strong passive candidates who weren’t actively looking for new roles.

    Each shortlisted candidate underwent a two-level evaluation process — a technical test and a cultural alignment interview — before being shared with the client.

    A single point of contact (SPOC) ensured seamless communication between our team and the client’s hiring managers. Feedback cycles were shortened to 24–48 hours, enabling a faster and more consistent pipeline flow.

    Result

    Closed five high-quality engineering hires within six weeks, meeting the client’s aggressive timelines and skill requirements.

    Success

    Established an ongoing recruitment partnership for future technical roles, enabling faster and more efficient hiring cycles.

    Streamlining the Shortlisting Process for a Manufacturing Company

    Requirement

    The client wanted to fill multiple mid-level positions across HR, Production, and Operations departments but faced delays due to manual shortlisting and inconsistent candidate evaluation.

    Challenges Faced

    • Over 300+ resumes received weekly with low relevance.
    • No standard process for resume screening or scoring.
    • Interview coordination delays between HR and hiring managers.

    How We Helped

    We started by analyzing the client’s existing hiring workflow to identify bottlenecks and inconsistencies. Based on this, we created a standardized shortlisting framework with defined evaluation criteria for each department.

    Our team introduced an automated resume parsing system to pre-filter profiles based on skill keywords, experience, and location. Recruiters then conducted a structured pre-screening call to assess communication, domain expertise, and notice period.

    To enhance collaboration, we developed a centralized candidate tracker that gave real-time visibility to both HR and department heads. Daily status updates ensured transparency and reduced communication delays.

    Additionally, we trained the internal HR team on using our evaluation templates, enabling them to maintain consistent shortlisting quality across all future recruitments.

    Result

    Reduced shortlisting time by 60% and improved interview-to-hire ratio by 40%.

    Success

    Improved the overall hiring experience for both candidates and managers, setting up a repeatable, efficient process for future recruitments.

    Fixing Low Offer-to-Join Ratio for a FinTech Startup

    Requirement

    The client aimed to hire skilled Sales Executives and Digital Marketers but faced frequent offer dropouts and slow onboarding.

    Challenges Faced

    • High offer rejections and dropouts before joining.
    • Lack of consistent follow-ups post-offer.
    • Candidates unclear about compensation and growth opportunities.

    How We Helped

    Our team conducted a detailed audit of the client’s offer and onboarding process to understand why candidates were backing out. We discovered that communication gaps and unclear expectations were the primary issues.

    To fix this, we implemented a candidate engagement program where recruiters stayed in constant touch with selected candidates from offer acceptance until joining.

    We created a personalized onboarding plan that included regular check-ins, company culture videos, and “Welcome Mailers” to keep engagement high. Additionally, we introduced pre-offer counseling sessions to clarify compensation, variable pay, and growth roadmap, reducing confusion and mismatched expectations

    For the client, we also set up an internal dashboard to track offer-to-join ratios and joining timelines, ensuring accountability and visibility.

    Result

    Improved offer-to-join ratio from 45% to 85% within two months and reduced joining delays by 10 days.

    Success

    Built stronger candidate relationships, leading to smoother onboarding and better retention for the client’s sales and marketing teams.

    Hiring Niche Roles for a Cloud Solutions Company

    Requirement

    The client needed to hire niche roles including Cloud Sales Executives and Solution Architects for a new Noida branch launch.

    Challenges Faced

    • Limited availability of qualified candidates in the local market.
    • Requirement for professionals with both technical and client-facing experience.
    • Short hiring window before the branch inauguration.

    How We Helped

    We began by understanding the specific skill mix required — sales acumen, cloud technology expertise, and solution presentation capability. Based on this, we created detailed candidate personas and sourcing parameters.

    Our recruitment specialists used LinkedIn Recruiter, cloud-specific job boards, and internal databases to target professionals from competitor companies and cloud service providers.

    We approached candidates through customized outreach messages emphasizing the growth opportunity of joining a new branch setup.

    Each candidate went through three evaluation rounds — a skill screening, a client sales scenario discussion, and a technical understanding interview — to ensure holistic fit.

    To support the client’s tight schedule, we managed all interview coordination, feedback tracking, and offer rollouts within a 30-day period, while keeping communication transparent through a single SPOC system.

    Result

    Closed three strategic hires — two Cloud Sales Executives and one Solution Architect — within one month.

    Success

    Enabled the client to successfully launch their Noida operations on schedule, with a strong, capable sales and solutions team driving immediate results.
